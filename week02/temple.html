Temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Aba Nigeria Temple

About the Temple

The Aba Nigeria Temple is a sacred building of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church), located in Aba, Abia State, Nigeria. It holds significant cultural and spiritual importance for members of the Church in West Africa.

Dedicated 7 August 2005: Dedicatory Prayer
